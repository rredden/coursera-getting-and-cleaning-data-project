The code book summarizes the data below that is the result of tidy.txt. 
It gathers the average for each variable for each activity for each subject.

Activity Labels - corresponds to what the subject was doing at the time of test measurement

WALKING (value 1): walking during the test
WALKING_UPSTAIRS (value 2): walking up a stairs during the test
WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS (value 3): walking down a stairs during the test
SITTING (value 4): sitting during the test
STANDING (value 5): standing during the test
LAYING (value 6): laying down during the test

Identifiers

subject - The ID of the test subject
activity - The activity being performed when the below measurements were taken
